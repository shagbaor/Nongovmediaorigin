Contented

Year: 2017

Location: River Benue, Nigeria

Category: Documentary

Along the banks of the River Benue, life flows at its own rhythm. Here, people draw their livelihood directly from the water — fishing, bathing, playing, even dredging sand and gravel to sell.

This series reflects on what it means to live close to the source — lives shaped not by excess but by what the river gives each day.
