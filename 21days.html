21 Days

Year: 2018

Location: Iseyin, Oyo State

During my National Youth Service, I documented the orientation camp at Iseyin. For 21 days, graduates were subjected to military-style regimentation.

This series captures the ritual of discipline—how a nation rehearses authority on its young citizens.
