21 Days

Year: 2018

Location: Iseyin, Oyo State

Category: Documentary Photography

In 2018, during my National Youth Service, I brought my camera into the orientation camp at Iseyin, Oyo State. For 21 days, graduates from across Nigeria were subjected to military-style regimentation...

At the center of it all was the RSM — with his ever-present stick tucked in his armpit, his booming voice, and his relentless orders.

This series documents not just punishment, but the ritual of discipline — how a nation rehearses authority on its young citizens.
