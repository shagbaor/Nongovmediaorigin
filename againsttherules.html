Against the Rules

Year: 2016

Location: University of Ilorin

Category: Documentary / Intimate Portraits

At the University of Ilorin College of Medicine, love was both everywhere and nowhere. Administrators discouraged it, and parents warned against it.

This series documents that quiet rebellion—young people negotiating first love while carrying the heavy weight of medical school expectations.

For me, the project was a way to explore intimacy as a social fact rather than a taboo. These stories exist, whether acknowledged or not.
