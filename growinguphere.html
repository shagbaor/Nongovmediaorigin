Growing up Here

Timeline: 2016 - 2018

Location: Nigeria (Makurdi, Ilorin, Ibadan)

This ongoing series is a collection of spontaneous portraits of children I've encountered in the neighborhoods I have called home.

What unites them is not just innocence, but resilience. Each child reflects the texture of their environment: the rough edges of the street and the bright flashes of joy.
