Growing up Here

Year: 2016-2018

Location: Makurdi, Ilorin and Ibadan, Nigeria

Category: Street / Documentary

This ongoing series is a collection of spontaneous portraits of children I've encountered in the neighborhoods I have called home.

What unites them is not just innocence, but resilience. Each child reflects the texture of their environment.
