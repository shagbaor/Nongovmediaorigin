Crossroads

Year: 2019

Location: University of Ilorin, Nigeria

This series was born out of necessity as much as passion. In 2018, I had just graduated with a degree in Anatomy. Photography, which had sustained me during school, suddenly felt out of reach.

I traveled back to campus to photograph portraits. These were not just graduation pictures; they were survival—a reminder that I could still earn through my craft.
