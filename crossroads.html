Crossroads

Year: 2019

Location: University of Ilorin, Nigeria

This series was born out of necessity as much as passion. In 2018, I had just graduated and was serving my NYSC year in Ibadan.

Moving to a new city was disorienting — no clients knew me, and work was scarce. Photography suddenly felt out of reach.

I traveled back to campus to photograph graduation portraits. These were not just pictures; they were survival.
