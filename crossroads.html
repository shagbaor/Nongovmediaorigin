Crossroads

Year: 2019

Location: University of Ilorin, Nigeria

Category: Portrait Photography / Documentary

This series was born out of necessity as much as passion. In 2018, I had just graduated from the University of Ilorin with a degree in Anatomy and was serving my NYSC year in Ibadan. Moving to a new city was disorienting.

When I heard that my juniors were having their convocation in Ilorin, I seized the chance. I traveled back to campus, camera in hand, to photograph their portraits.

Looking back, the images carry more weight than celebration alone. They document a community I had just left, the closing of one chapter and the uncertainty of another.
