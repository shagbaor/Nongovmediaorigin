Crossroads

2019 • University of Ilorin • Portrait

This series was born out of necessity as much as passion. In 2018, I had just graduated from the University of Ilorin with a degree in Anatomy and was serving my NYSC year in Ibadan.

Moving to a new city was disorienting — no clients knew me, and work was scarce. Photography, which had sustained me during school, suddenly felt out of reach.

Looking back, the images carry more weight than celebration alone. They document a community I had just left, the closing of one chapter and the uncertainty of another.
