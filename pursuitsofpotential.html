Pursuit of Happiness

Project: Editorial Series

Timeline: 2024 - 2026

Medium: Digital Photography & Film

This series explores the dichotomy between the external "hustle" and the internal search for contentment. It is a visual study of the quiet moments often overlooked in our race toward a defined success.

Captured across various urban landscapes, the work focuses on the intersection of light, architecture, and human emotion—capturing the "pursuit" not as a frantic chase, but as a deliberate walk through existence.

Every frame is an invitation to slow down and witness the beauty of the mundane, finding the sublime in the shadows of the city.
