Praise Singers

Year: 2018

Location: Yoruba Land, Nigeria

Category: Documentary Photography

In many Yoruba towns, events are rarely complete without the presence of elderly singers and drummers...

But beneath the rhythm lies an economy of survival. Each eulogy is offered with the hope of a token in return...

Through these images, I reflect on the delicate balance between heritage and necessity.
